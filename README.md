## Unbound'26 Hackathon
# 📚 EduVault

## Student Resource Sharing Portal

A centralized platform where college students can upload, discover, and share academic resources such as notes, previous year question papers, lab manuals, assignments, and presentations. EduVault eliminates scattered file-sharing across messaging apps and cloud drives by providing a structured, searchable repository that promotes collaboration and improves access to learning materials.

## Overview

In many colleges, study materials are scattered across WhatsApp groups, Telegram channels, Google Drive folders, and personal devices — making it difficult for students to quickly find what they need, especially during exams. EduVault solves this by offering a centralized platform where students can upload, organize, search, and download academic resources based on department, semester, subject, and category.

The platform encourages peer-to-peer knowledge sharing while ensuring valuable materials remain accessible for future batches. Features include secure authentication, advanced search and filtering, download tracking, ratings, and bookmarks — all designed to enhance collaboration and learning.

## Key Features

- 🔐 Secure user authentication
- 📤 Upload academic resources (PDFs, notes, lab manuals, assignments)
- 🔍 Search by subject, semester, department, and keywords
- 📥 Download study materials
- ⭐ Rate resources based on usefulness
- ❤️ Bookmark favorite resources
- 👤 User profile with uploads and download history
- 📊 Dashboard with resource statistics

## Problem Statement

Students struggle to find academic resources because they are scattered across multiple platforms (WhatsApp, Telegram, Google Drive). Files are frequently duplicated, misplaced, or lost over time, making collaboration inefficient.

## Solution

EduVault provides a centralized digital repository where students can contribute and access academic materials through a clean, searchable, organized interface — making resource sharing faster, easier, and more reliable.

## Why This Project?

- Reduces time spent searching for study materials
- Encourages collaboration among students
- Preserves academic resources for future batches
- Provides a structured, searchable repository
- Improves productivity during exams and coursework
